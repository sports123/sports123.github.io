  • Training regulary is very important because it can help you improve your skills and goal. At least train 3 to 6 times a week depending on your goal. For example, if your goal was to stand on the 3 point line and shoot 7 out of 10 balls in the hoop then maybe you can train about 4 times a week.
    		•
  • Set some goals so it can help you be focused on your training. Your goal doesn't need to be anything lofty and it should be something you can achieve. Such as 25 push-ups a day, 50 squats a day...etc.
    		•
  • Study movements from professionals so you can learn from them and make new goals to help you improve. For example, focus on how NBA players shoot, dribble and defend
    		•
  • Building confidence can help you reach your goals. If your goals are realistic then you will have the confidence to achieve it. You will perform better in tournaments with confidence because you believe you can do it and win nut if there is too much confidence, then you will just think you are the best on your team but you are actually not.
    		•
  • Be on a healthy diet so you can get fit and healthy. Stick to a diet only about 1,500-2,000 calories a day. This will help you to be fit and if your training includes a lot of sweating and working, then maybe consider a diet that is no more than 2,000 calories.
